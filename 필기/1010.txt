	       시퀀스명=ideabank_seq
4. 주요기능
	//service		//dao query
   4-1. 제안하기	-insert into ideabank values(ideabank_seq.nextval,?,?,?,defaut)

   4-3. 삭제하기	-delete from ideabank where num=?
   4-4. 검색하기	-select * from ideabank where title like '%?%'
   4-5. 전체보기	-select * from ideabank
